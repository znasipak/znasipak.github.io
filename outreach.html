Outreach

A summary of outreach activities that I have participated in and actively support.

Letters to a Pre-Scientist

Letters to a Pre-Scientist (LPS) is a non-profit organization that is focused on connecting middle school students in the United States with scientists across the world. Each school year, participating students and scientists get paired together according to common interests. We then exchange hand-written letters throughout the semester, in which we discuss what life is like as a scientist.

If you want to learn more about LPS, or are interested in signing-up as a future pen pal, click "Learn More" below.
